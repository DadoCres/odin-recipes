Caarbonara Recipe

Description

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an Italian favourite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

Ingredients:

  • Pancetta
  • Pecorino
  • Eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Chop pancetta and grate pecorino
  3. Beat the eggs
  4. Add spaghetti to boiling water
  5. Fry the pancetta
  6. Put grated cheeses in eggs
  7. Combine all together
